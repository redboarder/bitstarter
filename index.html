There is a lot about good food. Especially Italian food. People talk about it, taste it, write blogs. There is a huge variety of small manufacturers, artisans as they call themselves, who have slightly differently twisted recipe from their grandpa. And they exist in every single region, every small province and create their own tastes from the mountains in the north to the seashores in the South. But what can do a person overwhelmed with all that variety and often even not knowing that it really exists, that particular walnut torrone melted in a cup of freshly brewed espresso?

We bring the world of the Big Data to the sacred saint of life-being - enjoying your travel in life with food that perfectly suits your tastes.

  • First, we pass through different manufacturers and pick up samples of food they offer.
  • Second, using electronic tongue, we measure those samples to create a picture of the particular taste
  • Third, we offer it through our platform (basically, it is a kind of Italian food export effort ;) )

When you come to the site you see different characteristics of the chosen group of items. You want them sweeter, more bitter or slightly saltier than an average, you get it. After ordering and trying that delicious thing, put a grade of how much you like it. The more you try and grade, better we become in predicting what you may like and lead you to the food of your preference.

FAQ

Where does your food come from?
From a myriad of Italian small artisans.
How do you know my tastes?
We don't. With every your choice and every your grading, we learn your preferences.
